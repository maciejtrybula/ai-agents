# AI Search Optimization

## Focus

- Improve the probability that content is understood, selected, and cited by answer engines.
- Strengthen entities, factual precision, and source trust signals.
- Structure pages for direct answers, extractable summaries, and context-rich supporting detail.
- Reduce ambiguity in brand, product, author, and topic relationships.

## Workflow

1. Identify high-value questions, entities, and comparison intents.
2. Audit how clearly the site expresses facts, definitions, steps, and source attribution.
3. Improve machine-readable structure with semantic HTML, schema, and concise answer blocks.
4. Add differentiators that support citation: original data, expert quotes, examples, and named sources.
5. Define monitoring for AI mentions, citations, and branded answer visibility.

## Review checklist

- Key entities are explicitly named and consistently described.
- Pages answer likely user questions without forcing extra context lookup.
- Important claims are easy to quote and supported by trustworthy evidence.
- Structure helps retrieval systems identify summaries, steps, comparisons, and definitions.
- Brand and product positioning are unambiguous across pages and schema.
