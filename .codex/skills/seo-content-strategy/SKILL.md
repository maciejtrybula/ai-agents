# SEO Content Strategy

## Objectives

- Align page types and content clusters with real search intent.
- Improve topical authority through hub-and-spoke architecture and internal linking.
- Strengthen E-E-A-T with original evidence, authorship, sourcing, and freshness.
- Design scalable title, heading, snippet, and copy patterns for templates.

## Workflow

1. Identify the primary intents and audience jobs-to-be-done.
2. Map pillar pages, supporting pages, and conversion paths.
3. Review content gaps, cannibalization, and weak internal linking.
4. Improve headings, summaries, FAQs, and snippet-ready answer blocks.
5. Recommend editorial priorities and refresh cadence.

## Quality signals

- Primary query intent is satisfied early and clearly.
- Each page has a distinct role in the topic cluster.
- Internal links reinforce authority and discovery without over-optimization.
- Claims are supported by first-party expertise, data, or credible citations.
- Content is easy to summarize for snippets, AI answers, and featured results.
